# TT Score Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-28

TT Score Pro is a Wear OS table-tennis scoring app with an optional Android phone companion. This policy explains what data the app stores and how it is handled.

## Summary

- TT Score Pro stores match settings and active match state locally on your devices.
- If you use the phone companion, the watch shares live match data with the paired phone through the Wear OS Data Layer.
- TT Score Pro does not operate a developer backend and does not upload your match data to our own servers.
- TT Score Pro does not require an account.

## Data the app uses

TT Score Pro may store the following information locally on device:

- player names
- doubles player names
- match format and app settings
- active match state, including score, server, receiver, and set progress
- phone-side companion settings such as speech and jingle preferences

If the phone companion is paired with the watch, the following live match data may be shared between your paired devices:

- player and team names
- score
- current server
- current receiver
- set score
- match status

This device-to-device sync is used only to mirror the live match on your own paired devices.

## Speech and audio features

TT Score Pro can use Android system speech and audio features for:

- spoken score announcements
- serve and cue announcements
- result sounds
- speech input for player names on the watch

These features rely on platform services provided on your device. TT Score Pro does not provide its own cloud account system for these features.

## Data sharing

TT Score Pro does not sell your personal data.

TT Score Pro does not share your match data with the developer's own backend because the app does not use a developer-operated backend service.

## Data retention and deletion

Your app data remains on your device until you remove it.

You can delete locally stored data by:

- clearing app storage in Android system settings
- uninstalling the watch app
- uninstalling the phone companion app
